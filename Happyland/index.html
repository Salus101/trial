Happyland Tellapur (near Gachibowli) has classes from Jr KG to Grade 12.

Happyland is affiliated with both CAIE (Cambridge board) and CBSE. Happyland is recognized by CAIE to offer their curriculum from Grades 1 to 12 with the recognition number IN841. Happyland is recognized by CBSE to offer their curriculum from Grades 1-12 with the affiliation number 3630173. Until Grade 7, all children are taught using Cambridge methodologies. In Grade 8 children have the option of taking the IGCSE board exams (through Cambridge Board) or CBSE board exams.
